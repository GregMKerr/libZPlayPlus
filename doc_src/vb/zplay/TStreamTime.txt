Summary:
Stream time structure.

Parameters:
sec     - Total time in seconds.
ms      - Total time in milliseconds.
samples - Total time in samples.
hms     - Total time in <LINK libZPlayVB.TStreamHMSTime, TStreamHMSTime> format.

Remarks:
Use <LINK libZPlayVB.TTimeFormat, TTimeFormat> to specify which member in this structure is valid.
