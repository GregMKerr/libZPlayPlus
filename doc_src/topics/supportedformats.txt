Summary:
LibZPlay supports multiple audio formats. All decoders and encoders for these formats are included into libZPlay library.
You don't need additional library.

     <LINK FormatVorbis, OGG>  <IMAGE star_green>  <IMAGE star_green>  ogg              VORBIS OGG                                                   
     <LINK FormatFLAC, FLAC>   <IMAGE star_green>  <IMAGE star_green>  flac, oga        Native FLAC and OGG flac
